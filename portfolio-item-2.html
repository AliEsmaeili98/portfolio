ToDo application

A short subtitle

A simple ToDo application which is 'Task' based and helps you break your tasks to smaller parts!

The best way to do big tasks is to break them to smaller tasks so you don't feel overwhelmed. This app is designed to help u exactly with that! Basically you add a task and a description and then break it by adding todos and after u have done any of todos u can click it to mark it as done!

This app was one of my first Flutter apps which I could consider practical and I learnt a lot of new things creating this app!
